Aissatou Diallo

Postdoctoral Researcher

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Computer Science department at University College London advised by Anthony Hunter. I obtained my PhD at TU Darmstadt, Germany where I worked under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Johannes Fürnkranz at the Knowledge Engineering Group.

research interests

The focus of my PhD research was mainly on representation learning. Particularly, how to learn appropriate representation for complex data and or in non standard setting. I have also worked on analogical reasoning and learning to rank.
Recently, I started working on neuro-symbolic reasoning, program synthesis, particularly on program induction from natural language and more applied topics targeting the theory of repurposing.
